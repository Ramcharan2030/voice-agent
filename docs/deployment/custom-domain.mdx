---
title: "Custom Domain"
description: "Custom domain guidance for SPX Voice."
---

For new deployments, use [Coolify](/deployment/coolify). Coolify should own the
domain, HTTPS certificates, renewal, and routing to the `ui` service on port
`3010`.

The old host-level nginx/certbot deployment flow is not recommended for SPX
Voice because it conflicts with Coolify-managed domains and can overwrite fork
specific deployment files.

If you are not using Coolify, you may adapt `docker-compose.yaml` manually and
put your own reverse proxy in front of the `ui` service. In that case you own
certificate renewal, proxy headers, WebSocket forwarding, and firewall rules.
